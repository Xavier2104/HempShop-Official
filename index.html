About HEMPSHOP

HEMPSHOP is building Africa’s first dedicated marketplace for regulated hemp and cannabis products. Our mission is to connect verified merchants with customers across the continent, making it easy to discover and purchase high‑quality hemp‑based goods in a secure and compliant environment.
